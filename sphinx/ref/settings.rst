Settings
********

Here is a reference of the settings within the Alexandria configuration interface. There's a lot to cover

One thing to note is that some behavior may be the interactions of multiple things working together. For example, changing the configuration of the wireless interfaces may also involve changing the configuration of the dnsmasq or hostapd servives.
